FAQ

Common questions.

Are Knownly tools free?
Yes. The free tools and free diagnosis are designed to help users start with clarity. Deeper paid plans are optional and available when you want human-led support.
Which tool should I start with?
Start with the free Knownly Diagnosis if you are unsure. It connects your strengths, goals, content style and blocker into a clearer direction and feeds into all other tools.
Are these tools only for LinkedIn?
No. Knownly helps with online presence across LinkedIn, Instagram, X, YouTube, websites and other public profiles. Some tools focus on LinkedIn specifically, but most are platform-agnostic.
Do these tools guarantee followers or clients?
No. Knownly helps with clarity, positioning and opportunity readiness. It does not guarantee followers, clients, jobs or income. Results depend on your execution, consistency and context.
Can founders use these tools?
Yes. Founders can use the tools to clarify their founder-led voice, profile, content direction and trust-building strategy. There are also dedicated founder branding pages and services.
