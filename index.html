FAQ

Common questions.

Is Knownly a personal branding tool?
Yes, but it starts before typical personal branding. Knownly first helps you understand what you should be known for, then guides your profile, content and visibility direction.
Is the free report actually useful?
The free report is designed to give real clarity: your direction, strengths, blocker, content path and next step. Deeper paid plans are optional.
Does Knownly guarantee followers, clients, jobs or income?
No. Knownly gives clarity, positioning guidance and action direction. It does not guarantee followers, clients, jobs, income or viral growth.
Does Knownly scan private accounts?
No. Knownly only uses information you provide and public profile links you choose to share.
Who should use Knownly first?
Anyone who wants a stronger online presence but feels unclear about their direction, content, profile or positioning.
What should I do first?
Start with the free Knownly Diagnosis. It will give you your first direction report.
