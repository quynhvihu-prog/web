FAQ

Common questions.

Can I log in right now?
Not yet. Knownly accounts are coming soon. For now, your progress may be saved in your browser through localStorage so you can continue where you left off.
Will my report be saved forever?
During this prototype and early MVP stage, data is saved locally in your browser. A proper account system will be added later that will allow you to access saved reports across devices.
Should I start the diagnosis now?
Yes. You can take the free diagnosis and view your result immediately without needing an account.
Will accounts be free?
The basic account experience may start free. Deeper features and paid plans may be added as Knownly evolves.
