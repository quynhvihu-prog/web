Knownly Archetypes

Which of these feels closest to you?

Knownly identifies six direction archetypes. Each has a different public signal, content style and opportunity path. Your quiz result will point you to one of these — and your full diagnosis will sharpen it further.

Archetype 01

Trusted Educator

Core signal

Clarity and explanation — making complex things understandable for a specific audience.

Best for

Professionals in knowledge-heavy fields: tech, finance, health, law, science, education.

→ Creates: guides, breakdowns, myth-busting, explainers
Archetype 02

Practical Authority Builder

Core signal

Depth and specificity — being the most credible voice in a well-defined niche or industry.

Best for

Consultants, senior professionals, practitioners and specialists with real work experience.

→ Creates: opinions, frameworks, niche observations, case insights
Archetype 03

Silent Expert

Core signal

Trust through selectivity — deep expertise expressed quietly and without noise.

Best for

Introverted experts, researchers, advisors and those who prefer quality over frequency.

→ Creates: long-form, curated insights, selective thought leadership
Archetype 04

Strategic Opportunity Spotter

Core signal

Foresight — seeing patterns and market shifts before others and sharing that lens clearly.

Best for

Analysts, strategists, investors, business thinkers and trend-aware professionals.

→ Creates: trend pieces, predictions, market observations
Archetype 05

Founder-Led Voice

Core signal

Authentic perspective — a founder or builder sharing what they know from building something real.

Best for

Founders, entrepreneurs, early-stage builders and company leaders.

→ Creates: founder lessons, product stories, build-in-public content
Archetype 06

Creative Story Educator

Core signal

Narrative — teaching and sharing through stories, metaphors and experience rather than data.

Best for

Writers, coaches, creators, communicators and people who think in examples and stories.

→ Creates: stories, analogies, experience-based essays, narratives
