The Basics

What is a personal brand statement?

A personal brand statement is a short, clear sentence that explains who you are, what you help with, and what makes your perspective credible. It is not a slogan or a tagline — it is a working summary of your direction that you can use across your profiles, your bio and even how you introduce yourself in conversations.

When it is clear, people who read it should be able to answer one question quickly: "What should I remember this person for?"

The Formula

A simple formula that works for most people.

You do not need clever wording to start. A clear structure is more useful than a clever one.

I help [audience] understand / solve / achieve [problem or outcome] through [strength, experience or method].

Examples

Examples by audience.

These examples follow the same formula but adapt the language to different situations.

Job Seeker

"I help product teams ship faster by combining UX research with practical project management — built from 5 years across early-stage startups."

Freelancer

"I help small business owners get organized finances through simple bookkeeping systems, drawing on years of hands-on client work."

Founder

"I help operations leaders reduce manual work by building no-code automation systems, based on what I've learned scaling my own startup."

Consultant

"I help mid-size companies fix unclear hiring processes through practical, tested frameworks from over a decade in HR."

Coach

"I help new managers build confidence in difficult conversations through coaching grounded in real workplace experience."

Creator

"I help people simplify personal finance through clear, jargon-free explanations based on my own journey with budgeting."

Student

"I'm exploring data analytics and help peers understand core concepts through simple breakdowns as I learn alongside them."

Executive

"I help leadership teams navigate organizational change through frameworks developed across two decades in operations leadership."

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What is a personal brand statement?+

A personal brand statement is a short, clear sentence that explains who you help, what problem or outcome you focus on, and what experience or strength backs that up.

Where can I use my personal brand statement?+

You can use it in your LinkedIn headline or About section, your website bio, your Instagram or X bio, or your introduction in meetings and interviews.

Does my personal brand statement need to be permanent?+

No. Your statement can evolve as your skills, experience and goals change. It should reflect where you are now and where you are heading.

What if I help with more than one thing?+

Try to lead with your strongest or most relevant focus. You can mention secondary areas elsewhere, but your statement works best when it centers on one clear signal.
