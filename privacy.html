Privacy Policy

Your clarity should not come at the cost of your privacy.

Knownly is designed around consent-based inputs. We use the information you choose to provide to create your reports and improve your online presence experience.

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information we collect

Knownly collects only the information you choose to provide. This may include:

  • Your answers to the Knownly Diagnosis
  • Your name and email address
  • An optional WhatsApp number if you provide it
  • Your stated goals, interests, strengths and career stage
  • Public profile links you voluntarily choose to submit (such as LinkedIn, Instagram, X, YouTube, website or portfolio)
  • Messages submitted through the contact form
  • Pricing interest or early access requests
  • Browser-saved data through localStorage during the prototype and early MVP stage

2. What we do not collect

Knownly does not ask for passwords. We do not access private accounts. We do not ask you to connect private social media accounts. We do not claim to know everything about you. We do not guarantee outcomes based on your data.

3. How we use your information

  • To generate your Knownly Diagnosis result and direction report
  • To generate your Profile Report when you submit public profile links
  • To understand your positioning, visibility and online presence gaps
  • To respond to contact or early access requests
  • To improve the Knownly product experience
  • To share optional guidance if you request it
  • To understand which services users are interested in

4. Public profile links

Knownly may ask for public links such as LinkedIn, Instagram, X, YouTube, website or portfolio. These are used only if you choose to submit them. Knownly uses publicly visible content on these links to help generate your profile report context.

Please do not submit private links, passwords, private accounts or accounts you do not own or have permission to share.

5. LocalStorage and browser data

During the prototype and early MVP stage, some information may be saved in your browser's localStorage so pages can connect — for example, so your diagnosis results are accessible on your result page, or so your profile report request is remembered.

This is not a full account system. Data saved in localStorage stays in your browser and is not automatically synced to a server unless a specific form submission is made. You can clear this data through your browser settings at any time.

6. Third-party tools

Knownly may later use third-party tools for form handling, analytics, email, database management, AI model processing or customer support. When this happens, users will be informed through updated policies. We aim to use privacy-conscious tools wherever possible.

7. AI processing

Knownly may use AI models to help generate reports and structure insights from user-provided answers and public profile context. AI output is designed to be useful and thoughtful, but it is guidance — not a guarantee, a final truth, or a professional assessment.

AI output should be treated as a starting point for your own reflection and action, not as absolute fact about who you are.

8. Reviews, testimonials and case studies

Knownly will always request explicit permission before using user feedback, report excerpts, profile examples or case studies publicly. Anonymous examples may be used carefully and only with care for user privacy and dignity.

9. Data sharing

Knownly does not sell personal data. Information collected through Knownly may be used only to provide, improve or support Knownly services. It will not be shared with advertisers, data brokers or third parties for their own marketing purposes.

10. Your control over your data

You can request correction or deletion of submitted information by contacting Knownly. Visit the contact page to make a request. We will respond to reasonable data requests carefully and promptly.

11. Minors and younger users

Knownly is intended for users who are old enough to understand and consent to sharing career, goals, interests and profile information. Younger users should use Knownly with appropriate guidance from a parent, guardian or trusted adult.

12. Changes to this policy

Knownly may update this privacy policy as the platform evolves, new features are added or legal requirements change. Significant changes will be noted with an updated date at the top of this page.

13. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, concerns or data requests, please visit the Knownly contact page.
